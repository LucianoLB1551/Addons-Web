Education Edition Recipes V8.5 [Support V1.18 and V1.18 Bugs Fixed]

1.18.X

⚠ It may not work in latest versions. ⚠

Hi, I decided to create a more complete addon than the one I created several days ago Ballons+ this one only focuses on the balloon machine. I will be updating this addon with special tables, with interfaces and custom recipes to be able to obtain the survival education edition objects.

Welcome, this addon will allow you to create the things of the educational edition, each one is done in different tables with custom interfaces, I will show you below, the addon will be updated as you add more tables to create more objects, so stay tuned in case you are interested.

Added items (all items have recipes):

  • Mace

  • Tree bark (has all the variants of logs)

This addon adds the following recipes:

I try to balance the recipes so that you have a little difficulty and it is not all too simple.

Recipes on the CRAFTING TABLE:

Items:

Mace:

  • 6 Iron ingots
  • Stick

Iron plates:

  • Mace (you won't lose them)
  • Iron ingot

Pestle:

  • Stick
  • Cobblestone

Bark tree: (there is a variant for each tree)

  • Shears (you won't lose them)
  • Logs (you can use all minecraft logs)

Latex:

  • Tree bark (you can use any variant)
  • Water bucket (you won't lose the bucket)

Underwater torch:

  • Dust from heart of the sea
  • Torch

Colored torches:

  • Bright powder (you have 4 colors available)
  • Stick

Iron Stick:

  • 2 Iron ingots

Sparkles:

  • Glow powder (colors: blue, red, green, purple, orange(glowstone))
  • Iron Stick

Glow stick:

  • Glowstone dust
  • Dye (any color, except: light gray and black)
  • Latex

Blocks:

Balloons machine:

  • 2 Dirts
