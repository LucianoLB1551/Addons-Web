Paragliders [From Java to Bedrock] [Anim Fix v1.2]

1.20.X

1.19.X

If you want full-Breath-of-the-Wild-style paragliding action in Minecraft, this addon is for you. This addon adds...well, Paraglider and Deku leaf. A small porting of the java mod to bedrock. With love Red Ballon.

How do we start?

You have two options [PARAGLIDER OR DEKU LEAF]

Paraglider:


  • 5 Sticks
  • 3 Leathers

Paraglider:


  • Paraglider
  • Any type of leaves

Functioning:

  • Just having it equipped and being more than two blocks from the ground will activate itself.

Warnings for correct operation:

  • You must have a free ticking area space, if you have the 10 occupied areas it will not work.
  • If you had all the areas occupied, and you released one, you should put the following command /tag @s remove config2. for the addon to be reconfigured.
