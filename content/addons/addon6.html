Pickaxe Block!

1.19.X

Pickaxe Block you will be able to break several blocks at the same time in a fast and efficient way. Also, if you are a redstone lover, you can integrate this block with your creations to automate farms. Don't wait any longer and discover how the Pickaxe block can facilitate your adventure in Minecraft!

This addon adds a simple but very interesting block:

Pickaxe Block: (It looks like a dispenser maybe I will change the texture in the future)


  • Diamond Pickaxe
  • Dispenser
  • 7 Deepslate

Use:

Its use is very simple:

After placing it (you can place in any direction), the front part of the block will break the block in front of it.

To make it work you must place redstone on the opposite side of the block's front:

It only works with redstone dust, yes, you can activate it however you want (Buttons, levers, observers, pressure plates, etc.)

Depending on the redstone signal, it will break the blocks faster or slower:

Redstone Signal Seconds
0 -
1 3.75s
2 3.50s
3 3.25s
4 3.00s
