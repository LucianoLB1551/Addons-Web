MinePOP! [From Java to Bedrock] Attack On Titan Update

1.20.X

1.19.X

Do you want FUNKO-POP in your Minecraft world? Well, this is the right plugin for you. Straight from the Java edition ported to the Bedrock edition, I hope you enjoy it. Sword art update.

How do we start?

You must first create the Mine Pop X


  • Armor Stand
  • Any type of dye

Then you must go to the stonecutter, and choose the theme of the MinePOP


There are 11 themes:

  • DC Collection (9 figurines)
  • Demon Slayer (9 figurines)
  • Dragon Ball (9 figurines)
  • Marvel (9 figurines)
  • Minecraft (9 figurines)
  • Naruto (9 figurines)
  • One Piece (9 figurines)
  • Star Wars (9 figurines)
  • Sword Art (9 figurines)
  • Attack On Titan (9 figurines)
  • YouTube (1 figurine)

When you have chosen the theme, interact with the MiniPOP X, and it will give you a random one (everyone has the same chance), can you get them all?
