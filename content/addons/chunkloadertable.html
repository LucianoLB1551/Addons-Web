Chunk Loader Table

1.19.X

Have you ever wanted to keep certain areas of the game loaded even when you're not around? With our Chunk Loader Table, it is now possible. This awesome addon allows you to load specific chunks, ensuring that your important areas, automated farms, and construction projects stay up and running in your Minecraft world, even when you're far away! Find out how this chunk loading table can take your gaming experience to the next level.!

This addon adds 1 block and 11 crystals:

Chunk Loader Table:



  • Eye of Ender
  • 2 Blaze Powders
  • 3 Obsidians
  • Enchantment Table

Crystals:


  • 4 Glasses
  • Nether Quartz
Color Crystal Recipe

Use:

The use is very simple, you just have to place a crystal of any color in the chunk loader table, and that area will remain loaded.


The loaded area will be from the center of the block in a radius of 4 chunks ans the chunk loader table can be used in any dimension.

Warnings:

  • You can only have up to 10 tables in a world.
  • Each crystal color represents an area so you cannot repeat the colors.
  • although you can place tables in other dimensions, the maximum number of tables in the world is 10.
  • To see the tables in use that you have, you can use the /tickingarea list all-dimensions command, but you need to have cheats enabled, in a future update I will add a way to see the tables in use without having cheats enabled.
