Tired of walking, and that car or vehicle addons are not for survival mode or are obtained in a simple way, then this is your addon, I hope you like it.

Hello, welcome to my addon, with it you can create your cars as you want, for now it is not completely complete, but I want to know if people are interested to advance much more in this project.

The first steps to start:

All recipes are on the crafting table

  • You must take into account this construction to place everything you need:

  • The first thing is to create the workbench:
  • Known issues: For now the workbench can only be placed in one direction, this unfortunately is due to a limitation of Minecraft itself, since for some reason it does not allow commands with " " to be executed in block events.


  • You can interact with the ends of the car workbench, these act as a crafting table
  • After you create the workbench, you need to create the ramps for the vehicles:


  • Once you have the two ramps you must interact with them on the artboard:


  • When you are done with the ramps, you must get a smooth stone and interact with it on the table:


What's next now?

  • Now you must get the blueprint of the vehicle, you can do it by talking to a librarian: (JEEP Blueprint)


  • Price between 5 to 10 emeralds

Interact with the blueprint on the table:


Vehicles:

  • For now there is only one :(, but I will add more when the upgrade and customization system is complete for this one.

JEEP:

Well!, you are ready to start building, only the parts are missing:

Interact with bodywork on the workbench:


Cabin:

Interact with cabin on the workbench:


Doors:


Interact with door on the workbench:


Wheels:


Interact with wheels on the workbench (4 times):


It's time to choose the engine:

  • You have 3 levels, each one more powerful than the other, specifically 50% more powerful:

  • Level 1:

  • Level 2:

  • Level 3:

Just interact with the workbench with the engine you want:


Now you must choose the inventory of the vehicle:

    You have two options, the small or the large, but the latter eliminates 2 seats:

  • Small inventory:
  • 1 Chest



  • Large inventory:
  • 2 Chests



And the last ones you need, the key:


  • Flint
  • Iron Nugget

It is important that you have followed the steps in order of the parts, when you finish placing all, interact with the key and you can use your vehicle


You can carry the car if you run out of fuel, or get stuck (crouch down and interact with the vehicle, and do the same to remove it).


Because of the weight of the car you will have slowness 2.


Fuel system:

  • All vehicles after creating them have an empty fuel tank.
  • The higher level engines spend 20% more fuel compared to the previous level

There are 3 fuel levels:

  • Empty:


  • Full:


How to make fuel:

To start making fuel we need petrol, You can find it underground by mining between layer 14 and 0.


You can pick it up with a bucket, you will receive a bucket of petrol:


When you have petrol, you must do the refinery:


You can fill up to four buckets of petrol and put it to refine with lava bucket:


it takes 10 seconds to cook a quarter:


To extract the fuel you need a fuel can:


To refuel the vehicles you must bend down and interact with the vehicle, holding a Fuel Can with fuel in your hand (right click on PC, and keep the screen on mobile)

But this does not end here, you can automate the system a bit, with pipes and fluid tank.

Fluid tank:


You can store: water, lava, petrol, and gasoline.

Lava tanks and pipes now emit a faint light.


You can connect the pipes to the tanks.


RED: Extract fluid

GREEN: Enter fluid


You can make some systems with this:

You can connect a pipe that transports lava, above the refinery
